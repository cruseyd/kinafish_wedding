Memory Gardens

We’ll be having our event in downtown Monterey, at the historic Memory Gardens. Enclosed on three sides by tall adobe walls with graceful arched cutouts and on the fourth by the Spanish-Colonial façade of the historic Pacific House Museum, the small garden we will be celebrating in is within walking distance of over 10 different hotels, ranging from bed & breakfast to 4-star. Getting to Monterey

We know that spending the weekend with us in Monterey will be quite a trek for many of you. There are a number of options when it comes to getting to Monterey

Driving: There are two routes to Monterey by car, both very scenic. Highway One, which runs from San Francisco to Los Angeles, and Highway 101, which runs down the valley corridor.

Flying: Convenient Monterey Regional Airport is located just 10 minutes door-to-door from downtown Monterey. Visitors can fly direct to Monterey Regional Airport (MRY) via international and domestic connecting routes on Alaska Airlines, Allegiant, American Airlines and United Express. Fly direct from airports including Los Angeles (LAX), San Francisco (SFO), Phoenix (PHX), San Diego (SAN), Las Vegas (LAS), Denver (DEN) and Dallas (DFW). Other large airports near Monterey County include Norman Y. Mineta San Jose International Airport (SJC), located 60 minutes from downtown Monterey, and San Francisco International Airport is located 2 hours from downtown Monterey. Monterey Airbus and Main Event Transportation offer convenient door-to-door service to and from SFO and SJC.

Weather

We will be well shielded from the ocean breeze, but do note that our wedding will be taking place in late fall. Daily high temperatures range from 68°F to 65°F, rarely falling below 58°F or exceeding 77°F. Daily low temperatures range from 54°F to 50°F, rarely falling below 45°F or exceeding 59°F. The sun will set about 6 PM. Bring a sweater!
